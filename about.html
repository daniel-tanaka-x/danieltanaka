My Story

My journey into cryptocurrency and trading began with a fascination for technology and financial markets. What started as curiosity about Bitcoin evolved into a deep passion for understanding market dynamics, sentiment analysis, and the psychological aspects of trading decisions.

Based in Europe, I've developed expertise in Python programming, focusing on trading automation and market analysis tools. My projects range from simple trading bots to complex sentiment analysis systems that help traders overcome emotional decision-making—a common pitfall in volatile markets.

My breakthrough came with understanding that successful trading isn't just about technical analysis or market timing—it's about emotional control and systematic approaches. This insight led to my book "Don't Lose to Fear, Buy," which explores how sentiment analysis can improve investment decisions.

Today, I share my knowledge through Kindle books, technical articles on Qiita, and insights on Note.com. My goal is to make cryptocurrency trading and investment strategies accessible to everyone, combining technical expertise with practical, real-world applications.

My Journey

2008
The Beginning
Started writing seriously while working as a librarian. Completed my first novel manuscript after two years of early morning writing sessions.
2012
First Publication
Published my debut novel "The Midnight Garden" with Sterling Books. The book received critical acclaim and won the New Author Award.
2015
Bestseller Breakthrough
"Echoes of Tomorrow" hit the New York Times Bestseller List, marking my transition to full-time writing and establishing my reputation in sci-fi.
2018
Genre Expansion
Ventured into romance and mystery genres, proving versatility with "Hearts Entwined" and "The Silent Witness," both becoming bestsellers.
2021
Teaching & Mentoring
Published "The Writer's Journey," a comprehensive guide for aspiring authors, and began mentoring new writers through workshops and online courses.
2024
Continued Success
With 12 published books and over 500,000 copies sold worldwide, I continue to write, inspire, and connect with readers around the globe.

Fun Facts About Me

  • I write my first drafts entirely by hand in notebooks—I have over 200 filled notebooks!
  • My writing companion is a rescue cat named Hemingway who has strong opinions about plot twists.
  • I'm a coffee addict who owns 47 different mugs, each assigned to specific writing moods.
  • Before becoming an author, I worked as a librarian, bookstore clerk, and even a tour guide.
  • I've been to 23 countries and each trip inspires at least one character or setting in my books.
  • My guilty pleasure is reality TV—I find the interpersonal drama inspiring for character development.
  • I'm terrified of spiders but love writing about brave characters who face their fears.
  • My favorite writing spot is a 100-year-old oak tree in my backyard where I've hung a comfortable swing.

My Writing Philosophy

"Every story is a bridge between hearts. My job as a writer is to build those bridges strong enough to carry the weight of human experience, beautiful enough to inspire wonder, and wide enough to welcome everyone who wishes to cross."

I believe in the power of authentic storytelling to create understanding, spark conversations, and remind us of our shared humanity. Whether I'm writing about fictional characters or sharing insights on the craft of writing, my goal is always to connect, inspire, and entertain.
