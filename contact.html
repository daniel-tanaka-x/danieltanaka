Frequently Asked Questions

Do you respond to all emails?

I try my best to respond to every email I receive, though it may take a few days during busy periods. I read every message and truly appreciate hearing from my readers.

Are you available for speaking events?

Yes! I love speaking at book clubs, libraries, schools, and literary events. Please use the speaking engagement email above with details about your event.

Can you read my manuscript or provide feedback?

While I'm flattered by requests to read manuscripts, I'm unable to provide individual manuscript reviews due to time constraints and legal reasons. I recommend joining a local writing group or hiring a professional editor.

When will your next book be released?

I'm always working on new projects! The best way to stay updated on release dates is to subscribe to my newsletter or follow me on social media for the latest announcements.
